Course: Sequential Decision Making

Lecturers: Debabrota Basu, Riad Akrour, Hector Kohler.

Lecture Plan

  • Lecture 1 (28/11): Multi-armed Bandits (Lecturer: DB)

    Problem setup, Definition of regret, Lower and upper bounds (Why we prove them and their significances), UCB-type and Thompson Sampling-type algorithms

  • Lecture 2 (30/11): Contextual bandits (Lecturer: DB)

    Motivation from recommender systems, Problem setup (stochastic and adversarial), upper and lower bounds (why and what do they mean), UCB-type and TS-type algorithms

  • Practical 1 (05/12) (Lecturer: HK and DB)
  • Assignment 1
  • Lecture 3 (07/12): MDPs (Lecturer: DB)

    Problem setup, dynamic programming, Bellman optimality

  • Lecture 4 (19/12): Exact RL (Lecturer: DB)

    VI, PI, Q-learning, TD

  • Practical 2 (21/12) (Lecturer: HK)
  • Assignment 2
  • Lecture 5 (23/01): Approximate RL (Lecturer: DB)

    Functional approximation in MDPs, Projected Bellman operator, LSVI, LSPI, Fitted Q iterations

  • Practical 3 (25/01) (Lecturer: HK and DB)
  • Lecture 6 (30/01): Deep RL (Lecturer: RA)

    RL with deep function approximation

  • Practical 4 (01/02) (Lecturer: RA)
  • Assignment 3
  • Lecture 7 (06/02): Advanced Deep RL Techniques (Lecturer: RA)

    Policy gradient, Actor critics, Entropy regularisation

  • Practical 5 (08/02) (Lecturer: RA)
