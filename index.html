Introduction

I am Debabrota Basu, a postdoctoral researcher at the Data Science and AI Division, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Chalmers University of Technology. I work at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and optimization, focusing primarily on the design and analysis of algorithms for adaptive and online learning problems. My research aims to construct algorithms and systems for developing efficient, robust, private and ethical learning machines that solve real-life problems.

I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from the Department of Computer Science, School of Computing, National University of Singapore. Stéphane Bressan and Pierre Senellart have supervised my Doctoral thesis. I earned a B.E. degree with Honours in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Jadavpur University in 2014.

Outside academia, I write poetry and plays, air my dissent through articles on science and social issues, and have acted in plays and cinema. In order to know more about these activities, pass by the Arts and Articles page.
